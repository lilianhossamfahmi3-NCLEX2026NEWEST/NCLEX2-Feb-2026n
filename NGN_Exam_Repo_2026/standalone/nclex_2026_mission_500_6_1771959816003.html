The client is at risk for both bleeding and thromboembolic events due to the concurrent use of heparin and warfarin, and a subtherapeutic INR. Contacting the provider to increase the warfarin dose is necessary to achieve a therapeutic INR and reduce the risk of thromboembolic complications.
