A patient with a history of type 1 diabetes mellitus is admitted to the emergency department and diagnosed with diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA). Which intervention is the HIGHEST priority?

Item type [priorityAction] rendering in simplified mode.

✓ Clinical Rationales

Initiating intravenous (IV) fluid resuscitation is the highest priority in DKA due to dehydration from osmotic diuresis caused by hyperglycemia. Rapid rehydration helps restore intravascular volume, improves renal perfusion, and subsequently reduces blood glucose levels. Other interventions are dependent on this initial volume resuscitation.
