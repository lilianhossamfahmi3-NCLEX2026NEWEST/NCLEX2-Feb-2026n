A patient in the ICU has an arterial line and a central venous catheter in place. Which action should the nurse take FIRST?

Item type [priorityAction] rendering in simplified mode.

✓ Clinical Rationales

Assessing the arterial line insertion site for signs of bleeding or hematoma formation is the priority. An arterial line provides continuous blood pressure monitoring and frequent blood sampling, but it also carries the risk of bleeding. Early detection and intervention are crucial to prevent complications such as hemorrhage or compartment syndrome. Ignoring this could lead to rapid deterioration.
