✓ Clinical Rationales

Administer the N-acetylcysteine (NAC) loading dose as ordered: NAC is the antidote for acetaminophen overdose and helps prevent further liver damage. Monitor neurological status frequently: Elevated ammonia levels can lead to hepatic encephalopathy. Closely monitor for changes in mental status, tremors, and asterixis. Initiate bleeding precautions: Liver failure impairs clotting factor synthesis, increasing the risk of bleeding. Implement measures such as using soft toothbrushes and avoiding IM injections if possible. Prepare for possible transfer to a liver transplant center: Given the severity of the liver failure, the patient may require a liver transplant evaluation.
