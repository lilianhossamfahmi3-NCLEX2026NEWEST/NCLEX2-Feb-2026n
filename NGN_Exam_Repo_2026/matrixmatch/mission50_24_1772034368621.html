✓ Clinical Rationales

Heparin and warfarin have different mechanisms and onset of action. Heparin provides immediate anticoagulation, whereas warfarin takes several days to reach therapeutic levels. Initiating warfarin while continuing heparin allows for a smooth transition to long-term anticoagulation. An elevated aPTT indicates the heparin is therapeutic. An INR within the target range of 2-3 indicates therapeutic warfarin levels. Overlap therapy is continued until the INR is therapeutic for at least 24 hours. The patient should be monitored for bleeding during this time.
