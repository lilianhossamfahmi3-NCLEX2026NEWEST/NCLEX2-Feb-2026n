✓ Clinical Rationales

Inhaled vasodilators like Nitric Oxide or Epoprostenol (r1) selectively dilate blood vessels in ventilated areas of the lung, reducing pulmonary vascular resistance (c1). V-V ECMO (r2) is a 'rescue' therapy that takes over oxygenating the blood entirely, allowing the lungs to rest (c2). HFOV (r3) uses tiny tidal volumes to prevent lung injury (c3). Neuromuscular blockade (r4) is used to paralyze the client to stop them from 'fighting' the ventilator (c4).
