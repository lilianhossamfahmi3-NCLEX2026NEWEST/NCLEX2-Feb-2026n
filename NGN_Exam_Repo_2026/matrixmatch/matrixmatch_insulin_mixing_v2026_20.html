✓ Clinical Rationales

When mixing insulin, the sequence of air injection is: NPH (Cloudy) followed by Regular (Clear). The sequence of withdrawal is **Clear before Cloudy** to prevent contaminating the Regular vial with the protein in NPH. Vials should be rolled, never shaken, as shaking creates air bubbles and can damage the protein molecules. High-alert medications like insulin require a second nurse verification to ensure patient safety and prevent medication errors.
