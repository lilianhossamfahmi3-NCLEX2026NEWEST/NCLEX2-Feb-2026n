✓ Clinical Rationales

Fatigue, weight gain, and constipation are classic symptoms of hypothyroidism due to decreased metabolic rate. Bradycardia is also a common finding due to the slowed metabolic processes affecting the cardiovascular system. Cold intolerance is due to decreased thermogenesis, as thyroid hormones play a crucial role in regulating body temperature. Dry skin is due to decreased sweat and oil production, resulting from reduced metabolic activity in the skin cells. These symptoms collectively point towards a deficiency in thyroid hormone production.
