Defense mechanisms are unconscious psychological strategies used to protect the ego from anxiety and maintain psychological equilibrium. They can be adaptive or maladaptive. * **(r1) Displacement:** Redirecting emotions from the original source to a less threatening target. The client's anger towards the doctor is redirected to the nurse. * **(r2) Reaction Formation:** Transforming unacceptable impulses into their opposite. The client's dislike for their sibling is converted into excessive affection. * **(r3) Projection:** Attributing one's own unacceptable thoughts or feelings to another person. The client attributes their own lateness to their spouse. * **(r4) Rationalization:** Creating seemingly logical explanations to justify unacceptable thoughts or behaviors. The client claims they didn't want the promotion to avoid acknowledging disappointment.
