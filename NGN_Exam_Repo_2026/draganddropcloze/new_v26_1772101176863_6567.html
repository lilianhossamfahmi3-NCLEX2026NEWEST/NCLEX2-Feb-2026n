✓ Clinical Rationales

Fundal assessment frequency is crucial in the immediate postpartum period to monitor for hemorrhage. Assessing the fundus every 15 minutes allows for early detection of uterine atony. Massaging a boggy uterus stimulates uterine contraction, compressing blood vessels and reducing bleeding risk. Encouraging the client to void prevents bladder distention, which can displace the uterus and inhibit contraction.
