✓ Clinical Rationales

Administering propylthiouracil (PTU) helps block the synthesis of thyroid hormones, addressing the underlying cause of the thyroid storm. Propranolol is a beta-blocker that can help manage the symptoms of thyroid storm, such as tachycardia and hypertension. Applying a cooling blanket is crucial for managing the hyperthermia associated with thyroid storm. Monitoring the patient's EKG is important due to the risk of cardiac arrhythmias associated with thyroid storm. Administering IV fluids helps to combat dehydration, which can be exacerbated by hypermetabolism and fever.
