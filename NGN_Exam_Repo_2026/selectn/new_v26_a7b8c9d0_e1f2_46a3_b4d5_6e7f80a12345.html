✓ Clinical Rationales

Initiate continuous 1:1 observation to ensure patient safety and prevent further self-harm. This is crucial in the immediate aftermath of a suicide attempt. Communicate with the patient in a calm, non-judgmental manner to establish rapport and encourage expression of feelings. This helps build trust and facilitates therapeutic communication. Remove all potentially harmful objects from the patient's environment, such as sharp objects, cords, and medications. This minimizes the risk of impulsive self-harm. Administer prescribed PRN lorazepam as needed for anxiety, per physician's order. Anxiety can exacerbate suicidal ideation. Document the patient's behavior, statements, and interventions thoroughly and accurately in the EHR. This provides a comprehensive record for the healthcare team and ensures continuity of care.
