Tension pneumothorax is a life-threatening condition that requires immediate intervention. Key findings include absent breath sounds on the affected side, tracheal deviation away from the affected side, hypotension, hypoxia, and tachycardia. These findings indicate significant compromise of respiratory and cardiovascular function due to increased intrathoracic pressure.
