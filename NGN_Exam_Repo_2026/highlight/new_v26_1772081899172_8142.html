✓ Clinical Rationales

Administering a beta-blocker, such as propranolol, is the priority intervention to rapidly decrease heart rate and blood pressure, which are dangerously elevated in thyroid storm. This helps to prevent cardiac complications like arrhythmias and heart failure. Beta-blockers do not affect thyroid hormone levels directly but address the adrenergic symptoms.
