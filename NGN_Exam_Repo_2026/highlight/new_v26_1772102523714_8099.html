✓ Clinical Rationales

Decreased fetal movement can indicate fetal compromise, such as fetal hypoxia or placental insufficiency. A non-stress test (NST) is a non-invasive way to assess fetal well-being by monitoring fetal heart rate in response to fetal movement. It is the priority action to assess fetal status and determine if further intervention is needed. An NST helps to evaluate fetal oxygenation and identify potential fetal distress.
