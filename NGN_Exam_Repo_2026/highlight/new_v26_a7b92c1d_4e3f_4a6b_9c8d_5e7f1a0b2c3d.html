The combination of hypotension, tachycardia, tachypnea, oliguria, altered mental status, and elevated lactate are hallmark signs of septic shock. These findings indicate a systemic inflammatory response to infection leading to massive vasodilation, decreased tissue perfusion, and subsequent end-organ dysfunction. Immediate intervention is required to restore perfusion and prevent irreversible organ damage.
