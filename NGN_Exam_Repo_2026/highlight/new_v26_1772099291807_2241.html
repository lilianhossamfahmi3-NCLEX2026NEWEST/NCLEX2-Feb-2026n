Late decelerations indicate uteroplacental insufficiency, meaning the fetus is not receiving enough oxygen. This requires immediate intervention to improve fetal oxygenation. Actions include repositioning the mother to her left side to relieve pressure on the vena cava, administering oxygen via face mask at 8-10 L/min, increasing intravenous fluids to improve maternal blood volume, and notifying the healthcare provider immediately. Continuous fetal monitoring is crucial to assess the effectiveness of interventions.
