✓ Clinical Rationales

Administering desmopressin (DDAVP) as prescribed is the most important intervention. DDAVP is a synthetic vasopressin analog that replaces the missing antidiuretic hormone (ADH), reducing urine output and relieving polydipsia. This directly addresses the underlying cause of Diabetes Insipidus by replacing the deficient ADH, enabling the kidneys to reabsorb water more effectively. The medication helps to restore fluid balance and alleviate the symptoms of excessive thirst and urination.
