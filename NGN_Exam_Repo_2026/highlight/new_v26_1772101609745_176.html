✓ Clinical Rationales

Decreased fetal movement can indicate fetal compromise such as placental insufficiency or fetal hypoxia. The nurse should immediately assess fetal heart rate and activity via a non-stress test (NST) to determine fetal well-being. An NST monitors the fetal heart rate in response to fetal movements. Reactive NST (two accelerations of FHR in 20 minutes) indicates fetal well-being. Non-reactive NST requires further evaluation.
