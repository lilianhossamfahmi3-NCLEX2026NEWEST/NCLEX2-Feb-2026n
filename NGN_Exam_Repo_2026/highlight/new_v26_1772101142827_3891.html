A respiratory rate of 8 breaths/min in a COPD patient receiving oxygen is a critical finding indicating hypoventilation and potential respiratory failure. COPD patients are susceptible to oxygen-induced hypoventilation due to the suppression of their hypoxic drive. Immediate intervention is required, such as reducing or discontinuing oxygen and providing ventilatory support.
