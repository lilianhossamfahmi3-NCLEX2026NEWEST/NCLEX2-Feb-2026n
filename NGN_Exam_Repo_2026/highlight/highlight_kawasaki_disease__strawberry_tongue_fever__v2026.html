✓ Clinical Rationales

Kawasaki disease is an acute systemic vasculitis of unknown etiology that primarily affects children. Prompt recognition and treatment are essential to prevent serious complications, particularly coronary artery aneurysms. Strawberry tongue, persistent high fever, and hypotension are key indicators requiring immediate intervention.
