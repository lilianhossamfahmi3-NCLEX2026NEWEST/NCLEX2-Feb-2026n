✓ Clinical Rationales

Thyroid storm is a life-threatening condition requiring immediate intervention. Key findings that warrant immediate notification include: Restlessness and agitation (indicating neurological involvement and escalating crisis), a temperature of 104°F (40°C) (indicating hyperpyrexia, which can cause organ damage), a heart rate of 180 bpm (indicating severe tachycardia, compromising cardiac output), and BP 90/60 (indicating hypotension and potential shock).
