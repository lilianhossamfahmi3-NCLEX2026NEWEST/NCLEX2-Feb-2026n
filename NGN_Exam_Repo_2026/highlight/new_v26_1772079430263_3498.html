✓ Clinical Rationales

Massage the fundus. A boggy fundus is the most common cause of postpartum hemorrhage and should be addressed immediately with fundal massage to promote uterine contraction and reduce bleeding. Fundal massage stimulates the uterine muscles to contract, which helps to compress the blood vessels and reduce blood flow.
