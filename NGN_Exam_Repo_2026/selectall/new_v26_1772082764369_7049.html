✓ Clinical Rationales

The combination of fever, hypotension, elevated WBC, and elevated lactate is highly suggestive of sepsis. Lactate is a marker of anaerobic metabolism due to tissue hypoperfusion, indicating that the body's tissues are not receiving enough oxygen. This is a critical finding in sepsis and requires immediate intervention.
