✓ Clinical Rationales

Potassium, BUN, creatinine, and glucose levels are crucial in AKI. Elevated potassium levels can lead to life-threatening cardiac arrhythmias, particularly in the context of impaired renal function. BUN and creatinine are indicators of kidney function, and monitoring trends is essential for managing AKI. Glucose should be monitored, especially since the patient has diabetes.
