✓ Clinical Rationales

The priority interventions for acute pancreatitis focus on reducing pancreatic stimulation and managing life-threatening systemic complications. This includes making the client NPO to allow the pancreas to rest, administering aggressive IV fluids to combat hypovolemia from third-spacing, closely monitoring electrolytes for imbalances like hypokalemia and hypocalcemia, managing severe pain (a hallmark symptom), and monitoring respiratory status due to the high risk of ARDS.
