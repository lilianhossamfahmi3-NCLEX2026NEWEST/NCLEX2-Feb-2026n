✓ Clinical Rationales

Thyroid storm is a state of extreme hypermetabolism requiring multi-faceted intervention. The correct actions simultaneously block new hormone synthesis (propylthiouracil), control severe adrenergic symptoms like tachycardia (propranolol), reduce life-threatening hyperthermia (cooling blanket), manage profound dehydration from insensible losses (IV fluids), and monitor for cardiac decompensation (EKG monitoring).
