✓ Clinical Rationales

The appropriate interventions for HHS focus on a three-pronged approach: massive fluid resuscitation to correct profound dehydration, careful electrolyte management (especially potassium before insulin), and gradual reduction of hyperglycemia with an insulin infusion. Continuous cardiac and neurological monitoring is essential due to the high risk of dysrhythmias and cerebral edema.
