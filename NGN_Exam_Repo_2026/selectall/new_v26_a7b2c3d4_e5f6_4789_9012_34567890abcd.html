Administer furosemide 40 mg IV STAT: Furosemide is a loop diuretic that reduces preload by promoting fluid excretion, thereby alleviating pulmonary congestion and improving oxygenation in ADHF. Clinical Pearl: Monitor potassium levels as furosemide can cause hypokalemia. Clinical Pearl: Ensure patient comfort and prevent skin breakdown with proper positioning.
