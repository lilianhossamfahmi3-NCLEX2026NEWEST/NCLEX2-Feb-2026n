✓ Clinical Rationales

Desmopressin (DDAVP) is a synthetic analog of vasopressin, an antidiuretic hormone. Its effectiveness is indicated by a decrease in urine output, reflecting improved water reabsorption by the kidneys. This leads to a reduction in polyuria, a hallmark of diabetes insipidus. The goal is to restore normal fluid balance.
