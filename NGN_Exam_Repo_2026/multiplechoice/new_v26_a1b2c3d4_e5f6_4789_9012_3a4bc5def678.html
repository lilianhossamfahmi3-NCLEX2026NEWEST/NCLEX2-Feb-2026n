✓ Clinical Rationales

The correct rate is 16 mL/hour. To calculate the infusion rate, use the formula: (Desired dose / Concentration on hand) x Volume. In this case, (1600 units/hour / 25,000 units) x 250 mL = 16 mL/hour. Accurate medication calculation is vital for patient safety, especially with high-alert medications like heparin.
