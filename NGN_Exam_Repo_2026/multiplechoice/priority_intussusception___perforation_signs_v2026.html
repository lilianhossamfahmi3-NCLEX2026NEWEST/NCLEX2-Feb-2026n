✓ Clinical Rationales

The sudden onset of abdominal distension, rigidity, tenderness, absent bowel sounds, and guarding in a child with intussusception suggests a possible perforation. Perforation is a life-threatening complication that requires immediate medical intervention. Notifying the healthcare provider promptly is crucial for initiating timely treatment, which may include surgical intervention and antibiotics, to prevent sepsis and further complications.
