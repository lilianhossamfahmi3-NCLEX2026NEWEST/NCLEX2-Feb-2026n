✓ Clinical Rationales

Administering the STAT N-acetylcysteine (NAC) loading dose is the highest priority. In acetaminophen overdose, a toxic metabolite, N-acetyl-p-benzoquinone imine (NAPQI), accumulates and causes severe hepatocellular necrosis. NAC acts as a glutathione precursor, replenishing hepatic glutathione stores. This allows for the detoxification of NAPQI, halting further liver damage. Early administration is critical to prevent irreversible liver failure.
