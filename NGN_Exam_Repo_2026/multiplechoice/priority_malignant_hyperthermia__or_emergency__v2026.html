✓ Clinical Rationales

Dantrolene sodium is the specific antidote for malignant hyperthermia. It acts as a muscle relaxant by binding to the ryanodine receptor and decreasing the release of calcium from the sarcoplasmic reticulum, thus reducing muscle rigidity and slowing metabolism. Immediate administration is crucial to halt the progression of MH and improve patient outcomes. The faster dantrolene is administered, the better the prognosis.
