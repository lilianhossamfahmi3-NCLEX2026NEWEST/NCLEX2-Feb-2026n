✓ Clinical Rationales

Rapid cooling is the priority in heat stroke to prevent organ damage and death. Applying ice packs to areas with large blood vessels (groin, axillae) and using a cooling blanket facilitates rapid heat transfer and lowers the core temperature quickly. This intervention addresses the immediate life-threatening hyperthermia.
