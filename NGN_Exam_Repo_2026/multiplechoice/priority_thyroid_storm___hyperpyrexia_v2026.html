✓ Clinical Rationales

Aggressive cooling measures are the MOST immediate priority in hyperpyrexia (temperature >105.8°F/41°C) associated with thyroid storm. Hyperpyrexia can cause rapid cellular damage, neurological deficits, and death. While other interventions are important, lowering the temperature is paramount to prevent irreversible harm.
