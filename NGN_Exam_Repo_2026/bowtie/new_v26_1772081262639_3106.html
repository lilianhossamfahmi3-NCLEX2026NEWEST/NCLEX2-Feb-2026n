✓ Clinical Rationales

Sudden loss of bowel or bladder control can indicate cauda equina syndrome, a surgical emergency requiring immediate intervention to prevent permanent neurological damage. Clear fluid leaking from the incision site could indicate a cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) leak, which increases the risk of meningitis. New onset lower extremity weakness is concerning for nerve compression or damage, potentially leading to paralysis if not addressed promptly.
