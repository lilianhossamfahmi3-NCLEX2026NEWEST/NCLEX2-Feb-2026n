✓ Clinical Rationales

Prone positioning can improve oxygenation by redistributing lung perfusion and ventilation, reducing atelectasis. Maintaining a PEEP of 10 cm H2O is appropriate for ARDS to keep alveoli open and improve gas exchange. Titrating sedation is necessary to reduce patient-ventilator dyssynchrony and optimize ventilation.
